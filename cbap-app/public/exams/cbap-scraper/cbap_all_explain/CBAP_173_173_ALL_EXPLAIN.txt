Câu 1:
While assessing a change to a project requirement, Sianu is capturing the impact resolution. What is impact resolution?

A. An examination and resolution of the requirement relationships to determine which requirements will be impacted by a requested requirements change.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 This is the definition for Requirements Architecture, with a distracter of "resolution" substituting for "analysis." BABOK 5.4.5

B. A way to determine the formality of the assessment effort.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 This is the definition for Assessment Formality. BABOK 5.4.4.1

C. How all impacts and resolutions resulting from the change analysis are documented and communicated to all stakeholders.
   ✅ ĐÚNG
   📝 This is the definition for Impact Resolution. BABOK 5.4.4.3

D. Analysis performed to assess or evaluate the effect of a change.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 This is the definition for Impact Analysis. BABOK 5.4.4.2

➤ ĐÁP ÁN ĐÚNG: How all impacts and resolutions resulting from the change analysis are documented and communicated to all stakeholders.

🔥 GIẢI THÍCH CHI TIẾT (BABOK):
This is the definition for Impact Resolution. BABOK 5.4.4.3

Câu 2:
Which of the following considerations is NOT addressed when assessing the impact of a proposed change?

A. The business analyst evaluates the impact of any change to the dependencies between requirements.
   ✅ ĐÚNG
   📝 This is not a listed consideration to assessing a proposed change. Although this may be part of the impact analysis, the BABOK does not explicitly state that the analysis of dependencies have to occur when assessing a proposed change. "When a requirement changes, its relationships to other requirements or solution components can be reviewed." Watch for fine points like these to eliminate wrong answers. BABOK 5.4.4.2 first paragraph

B. The business analyst evaluates the benefit that will be gained by accepting the change.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 Evaluating the benefit that will be gained by accepting the change is listed as a consideration when assessing a proposed change. BABOK 5.5.4 bulleted list

C. The business analyst evaluates the impact of the change on customers and business processes.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 Evaluating the impact of the change on customers and business processes is listed as a consideration when assessing a proposed change. BABOK 5.5.4 bulleted list

D. The business analyst evaluates cost of the change, the cost of any associated work, and opportunity costs.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 Evaluating the cost of the change, the cost of any associated work, and opportunity costs is listed as a consideration when assessing a proposed change. BABOK 5.4.2 bulleted list

➤ ĐÁP ÁN ĐÚNG: The business analyst evaluates the impact of any change to the dependencies between requirements.

🔥 GIẢI THÍCH CHI TIẾT (BABOK):
This is not a listed consideration to assessing a proposed change. Although this may be part of the impact analysis, the BABOK does not explicitly state that the analysis of dependencies have to occur when assessing a proposed change. "When a requirement changes, its relationships to other requirements or solution components can be reviewed." Watch for fine points like these to eliminate wrong answers. BABOK 5.4.4.2 first paragraph

Câu 5:
What is performed to assess or evaluate the effect of a change?

A. Impact analysis
   ✅ ĐÚNG
   📝 Impact analysis is performed to assess and evaluate the effect of a change. BABOK 5.4.4.2

B. Baselining
   ❌ SAI
   📝 Baselining is completed before any requirements go through the process of assessing the change. Baselining is rarely mentioned in the BABOK--9.6 and Glossary only.

   ❌ SAI
   📝 Manage approvals occur after the change has been evaluated, rather than in evaluating the impact of the change. BABOK 5.5.4.4

   ❌ SAI
   📝 Traceability is a tool that can be used to conduct impact analysis, but is not something that is performed. BABOK 5.4.4.2

➤ ĐÁP ÁN ĐÚNG: Impact analysis

🔥 GIẢI THÍCH CHI TIẾT (BABOK):
Impact analysis is performed to assess and evaluate the effect of a change. BABOK 5.4.4.2
