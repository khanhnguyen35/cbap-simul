Câu 11:
You have decided to use the PERT estimating method for your BA activities because the sponsor is requiring a 95% confidence level. What is the formula for PERT?

A. (Optimistic * pessimistic) / 6.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 The formula for PERT is (optimistic + pessimistic + (4*most likely)) / 6. BABOK 10.19.3.1 and 10.19.3.4

B. (Optimistic + pessimistic + (3 times most likely)) / 6.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 There is an incorrect weight on the "most likely" value. BABOK 10.19.3.1 and 10.19.3.4

C. (Optimistic + pessimistic + (4 times most likely)) / 6.
   ✅ ĐÚNG
   📝 PERT weights the most likely estimate and can be used to provide estimates at various confidence levels. BABOK 10.19.3.1 and 10.19.3.4

D. (Optimistic + pessimistic + most likely) / 3.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 This is called the 3 pt. average and does not statistically weight the "most likely" value. BABOK 10.19.3.1 and 10.19.3.4

➤ ĐÁP ÁN ĐÚNG: (Optimistic + pessimistic + (4 times most likely)) / 6.

🔥 GIẢI THÍCH CHI TIẾT (BABOK):
PERT weights the most likely estimate and can be used to provide estimates at various confidence levels. BABOK 10.19.3.1 and 10.19.3.4

Câu 16:
Giewel is working as the business analyst on a compliance project. She has received estimates from all the involved stakeholders. She combines them all to get a total for all the activities and tasks. What method has she used for estimation?

A. Delphi estimation.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 Delphi does not ask for estimates from all involved stakeholders; rather, a panel of experts are asked for estimates anonymously. The facilitator of this technique then shares the estimates with the panel who revise their estimates; the process continues until consensus is reached.

B. Expert judgment.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 Expert judgement does not involve summing across all elements to provide an overall estimate.

C. Rolling wave.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 Rolling wave is not a sum but repeated estimates throughout an initiative or project, provides detailed estimates for near-term activities and extrapolates for the remainder of the initiative.

D. Bottom-up estimation.
   ✅ ĐÚNG
   📝 BABOK 10.19.3.1

➤ ĐÁP ÁN ĐÚNG: Bottom-up estimation.

BABOK 10.19.3.1
