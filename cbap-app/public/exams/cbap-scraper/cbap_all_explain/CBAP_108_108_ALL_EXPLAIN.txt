   ❌ SAI
   📝 A repository is simply a place to store documentation. BABOK 3.4.4.5

   ✅ ĐÚNG
   📝 Requirements attributes allow BAs to associate information with individual or related groups of requirements. BABOK 3.4.4.6

   ❌ SAI
   📝 Business policies define the limits within which decisions must be made; they do not provide the necessary information for this scenario. BABOK 3.4.5

   ❌ SAI
