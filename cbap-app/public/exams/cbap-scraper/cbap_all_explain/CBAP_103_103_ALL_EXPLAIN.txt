Câu 2:
Which of the following statements accurately describes stakeholders who would be engaged in the plan stakeholder engagement task?

A. Both customers and end-users are sources of external stakeholders.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 Customers are a source of external stakeholders, while end users are typically internal stakeholders. BABOK 3.2.7

   ✅ ĐÚNG

   ❌ SAI
   📝 Just the opposite. Suppliers are a source for external stakeholders. BABOK 3.2.7

   ❌ SAI
   📝 Both suppliers and customers refer to external stakeholders. BABOK 3.2.7

➤ ĐÁP ÁN ĐÚNG: A customer is a source of external stakeholders while an end-user is a source of internal stakeholders.

🔥 GIẢI THÍCH CHI TIẾT (BABOK):
End user is a term which typically describes internal stakeholders, while customer typically refers to external stakeholders. BABOK 3.2.7
