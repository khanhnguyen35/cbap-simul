Câu 1:
James is a business analyst who has gathered requirements for a new system to be implemented. His key stakeholder is Jennifer. When he reviewed the requirements with her, he sensed that Jennifer was not satisfied with the current requirements. She expressed that some of the requirements will not meet the needs of her department. The source of those requirements came from Jennifer's manager. What of the following choices should James do?

A. James should find a way to resolve this conflict either by facilitating a formal meeting, through research, or other methods.
   ✅ ĐÚNG
   📝 Resolving conflict while assessing the requirements should be handled by a workshop which includes all stakeholders to gain an understanding of the stakeholders' basis for prioritization or priorities in a group setting. BABOK 5.5.4.2, 3.3.4.1, and 9.5.4

B. James should ask Jennifer to talk to her boss in order to agree on what the requirements should be.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 This process is not very collaborative and does not involve the BA in the requirements process. The BA should never feel helpless and should seek a collaborative solution to resolving this problem. BABOK 5.5.4.2

C. James should escalate this conflict to another higher-level manager.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 Escalation should be the final step BABOK 5.5.4.2

D. James' hands are tied. After all, Jennifer will understand that her boss' requirements win out.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 The BA should never feel helpless and should seek a collaborative solution to this problem. BABOK 5.5.4.2

➤ ĐÁP ÁN ĐÚNG: James should find a way to resolve this conflict either by facilitating a formal meeting, through research, or other methods.

🔥 GIẢI THÍCH CHI TIẾT (BABOK):

   ❌ SAI
   📝 All stakeholder issues and conflicts must be resolved before baselining the project requirements. BABOK 5.5.1 and 5.5.4.4
