Câu 1:
Which key area has a set of tasks that helps ensure that the right people are involved in developing, understanding, and approving the project requirements?

A. Strategy analysis
   ❌ SAI
   📝 Strategy Analysis focuses on identifying the most effective way to apply the capabilities of an organization to reach the desired goals and objectives. BABOK 6.0

B. Business analysis planning and monitoring
   ❌ SAI
   📝 Business Analysis Planning and Monitoring organizes and coordinates the work efforts of the business analyst and stakeholders. BABOK 3.0

C. Solution evaluation
   ❌ SAI
   📝 Solution Evaluation describes the tasks performed to assess the performance of the solution, ensure value was delivered, and to remove any barriers that prevent maximum realization of value. BABOK 8.0

D. Requirements life cycle management
   ✅ ĐÚNG
   📝 The Requirements Lifecycle Management contains the tracing, prioritization, maintenance, change assessment, and approval of the requirements. The BA also works closely with key stakeholders to maintain understanding, agreement, and approval of requirements and designs. BABOK 1.4.2 and 5.0

➤ ĐÁP ÁN ĐÚNG: Requirements life cycle management

🔥 GIẢI THÍCH CHI TIẾT (BABOK):
The Requirements Lifecycle Management contains the tracing, prioritization, maintenance, change assessment, and approval of the requirements. The BA also works closely with key stakeholders to maintain understanding, agreement, and approval of requirements and designs. BABOK 1.4.2 and 5.0

   ❌ SAI
   📝 It is true that the BA role will continue to be performed within agile projects but tasks will not change; rather, they are approached and performed somewhat differently in agile projects. BABOK 1.2

B. Business analysis is a stated role; anyone who performs business analysis tasks can be considered a business analyst.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 BABOK 1.2. Business analysis is not a role. Business analyst is a role, but not business analysis, so this answer cannot be correct. Also, while the rest of the statement is true, it doesn't best address the scenario and related question.

   ❌ SAI
   📝 While there may not be an agile team member with the job title of "business analyst", the tasks of business analysis are critical and will remain so on all projects. BABOK 1.2

   ✅ ĐÚNG
   📝 BABOK 1.2 discusses agile as one of the perspectives in the BABOK; moreover, agile approaches are taken into consideration when planning the business analysis approach. BABOK 3.1.
