Câu 1:
Susan is a business analyst who is creating a process map of the current product changeover process on a manufacturing line. The sponsor advises her not to waste time on the current process because "everybody knows it doesn't work!" Instead, he counsels her to research a best practice and train everyone on it as quickly as possible. What is Susan's best course of action?

A. Respectfully explain that the current process will be documented and analyzed just to the point of identifying what exactly will need to change so the effects of the change can be assessed.
   ✅ ĐÚNG
   📝 The current state should be explored in just enough detail to validate the need of the change, identify exactly what needs to change to achieve a desired future state, and analyze its effects. BABOK 6.1.2

B. Susan should quickly develop a cost/benefit analysis of pursuing her current course of action to demonstrate the overall value to her sponsor.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 This may be necessary at some level depending upon the sponsor's communication and decision-making style but is not the most correct answer. BABOK 6.1.2

C. Agree that in this situation it makes sense to research best practice and implement it as soon as possible to maximize business value.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 Analyzing the current state is necessary to maximize value in the future state. BABOK 6.1.2

D. Agree with the sponsor to minimize conflict but continue process mapping as a tool to use in analysis of the current state.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 This may minimize potential conflict with the sponsor but business analysis tasks, activities, and resulting artifacts should be communicated (transparent). BABOK 6.1.2

➤ ĐÁP ÁN ĐÚNG: Respectfully explain that the current process will be documented and analyzed just to the point of identifying what exactly will need to change so the effects of the change can be assessed.

🔥 GIẢI THÍCH CHI TIẾT (BABOK):
The current state should be explored in just enough detail to validate the need of the change, identify exactly what needs to change to achieve a desired future state, and analyze its effects. BABOK 6.1.2

Câu 2:
Farouk is a business analyst working with a business owner to define the business requirements related to a new opportunity in his organization. He works in a company that produces bathroom fixtures. A. Farouk needs to understand the cost of doing nothing; in other words, not pursuing the opportunity.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 Farouk does need to understand the consequences of maintaining the status quo but that pertains more to understanding the business need vs. business requirements. BABOK 6.1.8

   📝 The word "thorough" is key here as business requirements are a primary output for the analyze current state task. Understanding constraints is also specifically mentioned in the definition of business requirements. BABOK 6.1.8

D. Farouk will focus on understanding why the change is needed along with expected benefits from pursuing the opportunity.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 This information is absolutely needed to understand business requirements but this answer pertains more to the business need than business requirements. BABOK 6.1.8

➤ ĐÁP ÁN ĐÚNG: Farouk needs a thorough understanding of the opportunity and possible benefits given organizational constraints.

Câu 3:
What is the best description of the strategy analysis knowledge area?

A. The strategy analysis knowledge area begins when a business need is identified, analyzes the current state, and enables the enterprise to effectively address that need by defining the future and transition states.
   ✅ ĐÚNG
   📝 This is the correct definition of the strategy analysis knowledge area. BABOK 6.0

B. Strategy analysis consists of the collection of pre-project activities for capturing future views of the business to provide context for Elicitation.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 Strategy analysis is often the starting point for initiating a new project, but continues as changes occur and new information becomes available. It is through strategy analysis that business requirements are identified and documented. BABOK 6.0

   ❌ SAI
   📝 Strategy analysis is an ongoing activity that assesses any changes in the business need or continues as new information becomes available that may indicate a modification to the change strategy may be required. BABOK 6.0

   ❌ SAI
   📝 Understanding the current state is only one aspect of strategy analysis. BABOK 6.0

➤ ĐÁP ÁN ĐÚNG: The strategy analysis knowledge area begins when a business need is identified, analyzes the current state, and enables the enterprise to effectively address that need by defining the future and transition states.
