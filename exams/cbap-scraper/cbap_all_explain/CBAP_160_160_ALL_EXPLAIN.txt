   ❌ SAI

   ❌ SAI

Câu 2:
Susan thinks she is done with her requirements analysis on a project. Her project manager sent her a terse email telling her she is not done yet. He also sent her the business objectives and potential value for the project, and asked her to complete one more important task to ensure the requirements support the business case. What activity does Susan need to complete?

A. Identify any missing or incorrect requirements.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 An invented answer, and the closest task is requirements verification which does not use the business objectives and potential value as guidelines.

B. Requirements verification.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 Requirements verification ensures requirements are of sufficient quality to be able to begin construction. BABOK 7.2.1

C. Obtain approval of the requirements.
   ❌ SAI
   📝 Obtaining requirements approval is concerned with management and maintenance of requirements and design. The question is concerned with business value. Further, it does not use the business objectives and potential value as guidelines.

D. Requirements validation.
   ✅ ĐÚNG
   📝 Requirements validation helps ensure that requirements provide business value and meet business needs. BABOK 7.3.1

➤ ĐÁP ÁN ĐÚNG: Requirements validation.

🔥 GIẢI THÍCH CHI TIẾT (BABOK):
Requirements validation helps ensure that requirements provide business value and meet business needs. BABOK 7.3.1

Câu 3:
You have interviewed key stakeholders to uncover their business needs. You become aware of many factors that the stakeholders think are true, but which have not been verified. You decide to document which of the following:

A. To-be processes
   ❌ SAI
   📝 These are not factors believed to be true, these are desired future processes.

B. Assumptions
   ✅ ĐÚNG
   📝 Assumptions are factors that are believed to be true but has not been confirmed to be accurate, or that could be true now but may not be in the future. BABOK 7.3.4.1 and Glossary

C. Requirements
   ❌ SAI
   📝 Per the BABOK, a requirement is are "a usable representation of a need that is known and true at the time of the request. BABOK Glossary.

D. Constraints
   ❌ SAI
   📝 Constraints limit what a project or solution can do.

➤ ĐÁP ÁN ĐÚNG: Assumptions

🔥 GIẢI THÍCH CHI TIẾT (BABOK):
Assumptions are factors that are believed to be true but has not been confirmed to be accurate, or that could be true now but may not be in the future. BABOK 7.3.4.1 and Glossary

   ❌ SAI
