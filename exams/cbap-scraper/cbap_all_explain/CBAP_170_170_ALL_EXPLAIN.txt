Câu 1:
Three of the following statements are inaccurate about prioritization. Which one is accurate?

A. Stakeholders may intentionally indicate priority to influence the result to their desired outcome.
   ✅ ĐÚNG
   📝 This is one of the challenges of prioritization which is an element of the prioritize requirements task. BABOK 5.3.4.2

   ❌ SAI

   ❌ SAI
   📝 Prioritization is an assessment of relative value as each stakeholder may have different values. BABOK 5.3.4.2

   ❌ SAI
   📝 Priorities may shift as context evolves. BABOK 5.3.4.3

🔥 GIẢI THÍCH CHI TIẾT (BABOK):
This is one of the challenges of prioritization which is an element of the prioritize requirements task. BABOK 5.3.4.2

Câu 5:
