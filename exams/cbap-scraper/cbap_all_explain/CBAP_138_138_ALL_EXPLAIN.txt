   ❌ SAI
   📝 The BABOK makes no mention of sponsor approvals. BABOK 4.4.4.1

   ❌ SAI

   📝 This is the correct description of formal documentation. In addition, informal documentation may include text, diagrams, or matrices (just like formal documentation) but they are not part of a formal organizational process. BABOK 4.4.4.1
